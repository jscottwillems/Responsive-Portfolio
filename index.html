About

Josh Willems is currently a student at the Houston Coding Bootcamp by UT. He was born in Morristown, New Jersey in April of 1988. He moved with his family to the Houston area at an early age and graduated from Clear Lake High School in 2006. He currently works as a retail auditor for Riggins Inventory Services LLC. In his free time he enjoys playing guitar, hiking and spending time with his family. He recently found interest in web development and decided to kindle that fire by returning to school.


After graduation he intends to focus his energy on breaking into the vast world of professional web development in hopes of embarking on a life-long career path that will not only allow him to grow professionally, but also grant him the opportunity to continue to aquire new skills and overcome new challenges.
